About Me

I’m passionate about development and love to see ideas come to life. I have nearly 15 years of development experience spanning from native iOS & Android mobile applications, databases, web sites & API’s, to desktop applications. I strive to architect modern & scalable solutions.

I believe in building it right the first time. I steer clear of 'band–aid' approaches that only cause later headaches. I code in Swift, Objective–C, Java, C#, .Net, Ruby and use open source software. I keep up to date with the latest industry trends and SDK updates.

Personally, I live in the Denver / Boulder metro area and enjoy skiing, snow, traveling, the outdoors, weather and, last but not least, coding.
